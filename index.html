My bak-bak cutie

how simple she looks

She says she doesn't understand anything

how crazy it is inside

sometimes strange and sometimes beautiful

Sometimes it seems like a scene from a book

Where have I come? Tell me how kind this is.

Someone's heart belongs to her, she has no control over it.

What should I do if I do, what should I say if I say?

This poem is also an excuse to bring her closer to my heart.
